Smashtron-5K6 by Smashtron

A game of strength

Smashtron5k(6) from Nicholas Scott Spencer on Vimeo.

Smashtron 5K6 is a two player game of strength. Use this sketch, along with Sparkfun's SerLCD and two Force Sensitive Resistors to create a game where player see who can apply the most pressure over time.

Dependencies

Arduino (any should work) Sparkfun's SerLCD 2x Force Sensitive Resitors (FSRs) 2x 10K resistors to ground the FSRs Some wire
